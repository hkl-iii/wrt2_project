Jesus Christ of Nazareth

Jesus Christ is the founding figure of Christianity's New Testament.

About Jesus Christ

  • Birth was prophesied when his virgin mother Mary was visited by the Angel Gabriel
  • Was born in Bethlehem between 6BC-4BC. But grew up in Nazareth.
  • Claimed to be the Son of God himself.
  • Is the cousin of John the Baptist
  • The years of Jesus' life between age 12 and 30 are known as the Lost Years of Jesus.
  • Reappeared at the age of 30. Could perform miracles (heal the sick, raise the dead, exorcise evil spirits, etc.
  • Taught wisdom in parables. Eventually gained 12 disciples
  • Teachings were considered controversial because of the large growth of his following.
  • Was set up and betrayed by disciple Judass
  • Was falsely accused of crimes and crucified on the cross outside of Jerusalem.
  • Buried in a tomb in Golgotha, the place of skulls.
  • It is said that he resurrected from the tomb three days later.
